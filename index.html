Nissan 370z

By Nissan Motor Company

Information

The Nissan 370Z (known as the Fairlady Z Z34 in Japan) is a 2-door, 2-seater sports car (S-segment in Europe) manufactured by Nissan Motor Company.

Specs

  • 3.7-liter V6 engine
  • 332 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque
  • 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission
  • Rear-wheel drive and limited-slip differential
  • 0 to 60 mph in around 5 seconds
  • Top speed of 155 mph
  • Coupe version weighs around 3,333 pounds
  • Roadster version weighs around 3,580 pounds
